MathStudyingTexts

This is an open source page containing some texts of courses in mathematics.

Author

The author and the owner of the page is Zheng Wai Hong, an underguadrate student of School of Mathematical Science, Zhejiang University. You can contact to Zheng by email: waihongzhg@gmail.com

Vision

The author Zheng, as an undergraduate student obsessed with math, notice that most of the math textbooks in colleges are relatively disorganized to the extent that quite a few students find them difficult, including the author.
Therefore, Zheng decided to build a website about professional mathematical educations with uniform notations, clear logic and being easily available. Indeed, the idea was inspired by MIT OpenCourseWare, which is an open website providing the educational materials of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).
This website is open source and the original codes and contents will be stored in the corresponding github repository. (I will put it later)
